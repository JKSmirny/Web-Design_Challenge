Latitude project

  • What is Latitude?

    • The goal of this projectwas to analyze what impact latitude has on weather conditions: temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed. Thea data were uploaded from the OpenWeatherMapAPI.

    Matplotlib was used to plot the data. This webpage provides the source data and visualizations as well as descriptions of trends and correlations.
